We Offer

Skin Treatments


We treat various skin concerns, including acne, anti-aging issues, pigmentation problems, and scars. We also manage clinical dermatological disorders such as eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, and dermatitis. Skin cancer screenings and biopsies are performed, alongside non-invasive skin tightening treatments.

Hair and Nail Treatments


We treat hair issues like hair loss, alopecia, and scalp conditions with microneedling, PRP therapy, medications and laser treatments. For nails, we address fungal infections, psoriasis, ingrown nails, and other disorders with medications and minor procedures.

Laser & other Treatments

Laser & other Treatments
We use laser treatments to address various skin concerns such as acne scars, pigmentation, wrinkles, and hair removal. We also offer non-laser treatments like chemical peels, microneedling, PRP, mesotherapy, procedures like mole/cysts/wart removals and anti-aging treatments like botox, fillers and threads.

Modern Facilities

Our clinic is equipped with state-of-the-art tools to provide cutting-edge treatments in a relaxing environment.
