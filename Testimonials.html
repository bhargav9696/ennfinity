About Us

Welcome to Ennfinity Skin, where your skin health is our top priority. At our state-of-the-art dermatology clinic, we are committed to providing a range of cutting-edge Clinical and Cosmetic Dermatological solutions tailored to your unique needs.

We address a wide range of skin concerns, including diagnosing and treating disorders related to the skin, hair, nails, and mucous membranes. We combine expert medical care with advanced aesthetic treatments to address all your skin concerns. Whether you're seeking solutions for acne, eczema, aging, pigmentation, or looking to enhance your natural beauty with non-invasive procedures like Botox or dermal fillers, we have the expertise and solutions you need.

About Dr. Ennakshee

Dr. Ennakshee (MBBS, MD Dermatology) is a highly skilled and compassionate dermatologist with a deep passion for skin care. She offers both clinical and aesthetic dermatology services to address a wide range of skin concerns, from medical conditions like acne, eczema, and psoriasis to cosmetic procedures like Peels, Botox, threads, and fillers.

With extensive training and experience, Dr. Ennakshee combines advanced techniques with personalized care to help her patients achieve healthy, glowing skin. Her commitment to staying up-to-date with the latest dermatological advancements ensures that each patient receives the best possible treatment.
